    Welcome to my professional portfolio! I'm Aidan Andrews, a passionate technologist, entrepreneur, and academic, currently pursuing advanced studies in AI, machine learning, and physics at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. My journey is not just about acquiring knowledge but also about applying it to create meaningful impact.

    As the founder and CEO of Nvralone, I've navigated the path to early profitability while infusing our mission with a commitment to mental health. This endeavor showcases my belief in technology's potential to improve lives beyond just the realms of business and academia.

    My technical expertise is extensive and ever-growing. I am skilled in languages like Java, Python, and Kotlin, and I have hands-on experience in developing machine learning-driven applications and solving complex algorithmic challenges. This technical proficiency is balanced by my leadership experiences, ranging from guiding athletic and academic teams to driving tech initiatives.

    I am studying a Bachelor of Science in Physics (Computer Science Track) with a dual degree in ILEE and concurrently working towards a Master of Science in Computer Science, both from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. My academic excellence is reflected in my current GPAs (3.8 in undergraduate and 4.0 in computer science studies).

    My entrepreneurial journey with Nvralone has been a blend of technology and business acumen, leading projects from concept to market. This has involved aspects like web development, financial management, marketing, and product development. Furthermore, my extracurricular activities include being a member of SIGAIDA, where I've researched and implemented deep learning models, and my time as a Division 1 Level Ice Hockey Player, which honed my teamwork and strategic thinking skills.

    My project portfolio is diverse, ranging from developing a Devanagari Language Learning Web App to VoxAI, an innovative autonomous learning assistant. These projects highlight my ability to integrate machine learning models into practical applications and develop full-stack solutions.

    In terms of technical skills, I am proficient in a range of languages and tools, including Java, Python, C/C++, Kotlin, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, TensorFlow, Keras, scikit-learn, and various developer tools like Git, Linux, and Google Cloud Platform.
